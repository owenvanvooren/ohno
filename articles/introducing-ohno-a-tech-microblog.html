ohno: a tech microblog

A weekly (fingers crossed), tiny blog about technology

Published on Friday, May 30

The world of tech is constantly evolving.
Every single day, there are wild new things happening in the tech industry that no one saw coming.

I'll post a new article every week to unwrap what's happening in the world of tech and how it affects you, plus some hot takes of my own.

I'm hoping to release an update every Friday, but stay tuned for scheduling updates, my summer's gonna be busy!
If you've got any questions, comments, or ideas, feel free to get in touch at hi@owen.uno.
See you soon!

P.S. If there's anything I know about the tech universe, it's that things can go south real quick. That's why the blog is called “ohno”! Also, it's short, it's fun, and it's a little bit quirky, which makes it memorable. Thanks for checking out the blog!
