ohno: a tech microblog

A weekly (fingers crossed), tiny blog about technology

Published on Friday, May 30

The world of tech is constantly evolving.
Every single day, there are wild new things happening in the tech industry that no one saw coming.

I'll be here every week to keep you in the loop about what's happening and how it affects you, plus some hot takes of my own.

I'm hoping to release a new article/post every week, but stay tuned for scheduling updates, my summer's gonna be busy!
If you've got any questions, comments, or ideas, feel free to get in touch at hi@owen.uno.
See you soon!

P.S. If there's anything I know about the tech universe, it's that things can go south real quick. That's why the blog is called “ohno”. Also, it's fun, informal, and interesting, which makes it memorable.
